Incognito Mod

Download Incognito Mod v1.1 for Minecraft 1.8.9

The Incognito Mod allows you to hide nametags, holograms, the chat, and the sidebar with just one button.

To hide the sidebar, Sidebar Mod Revamp by powns is required

Forge is required to use this mod



Changelog for v1.1:

  • Fixed a bug where Incognito would activate itself upon startup regardless of user preference
  • Added a update checker
